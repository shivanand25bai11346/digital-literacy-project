1. Project Overview

This project focuses on developing essential digital skills required for academic, professional,
and everyday life in today’s technology-driven world. As a Student Digital Ambassador, the
objective is to understand and promote responsible digital habits among peers.
The project is divided into five key modules, each addressing an important aspect of digital
literacy:

• Awareness of digital issues

• Building an online profile

• Using coding and collaboration tools

• Professional online communication

• Cyber safety and security

These modules combine practical activities with real-life applications to create a strong digital
foundation.

Through this project, students explore platforms such as GitHub, LinkedIn, and coding
websites, while also learning ethical and responsible online behavior. The project not only
enhances technical exposure but also emphasizes professionalism, collaboration, and security in
the digital space.

---
2. Objectives of the Project
---
• To develop awareness about digital literacy and its importance

• To build a professional online presence

• To improve problem-solving and collaboration skills

• To enhance formal communication abilities

• To understand cyber threats and preventive measures

---
Module Summaries
---

Module 1: Digital Literacy Awareness
This module introduces the concept of digital literacy and its significance in a student’s life. It
involves creating a visual infographic covering:
• Safe internet practices
• Useful digital tools
• Maintaining a professional online presence
It enhances creativity and the ability to present information effectively. The module also
promotes responsible internet usage and highlights the importance of digital literacy for
academic and career growth.


Module 2: Student Digital Portfolio
This module focuses on building a professional online identity using platforms like:
• GitHub
• LinkedIn
Students create and organize their profiles with academic and career-related details. It introduces
personal branding and helps in long-term career planning by making profiles accessible to
recruiters and collaborators.


Module 3: Coding & Collaboration Platforms
This module provides hands-on experience with:
• Coding platforms (basic problem solving)
• Cloud-based tools (e.g., Google Forms)
It improves logical thinking, problem-solving skills, and familiarity with real-world tools. The
module also emphasizes teamwork and effective digital collaboration.


Module 4: Professional Communication & Etiquette
This module develops formal communication skills, including:
• Writing professional emails
• Following social media etiquette
It ensures students can communicate clearly and respectfully in academic and professional
environments while maintaining a positive digital reputation.


Module 5: Cybercrime Awareness & Prevention
This module focuses on:
• Types of cybercrimes
• Preventive measures
• Awareness strategies
Students analyze scenarios and create safety checklists. It helps in identifying online threats,
understanding consequences, and adopting secure digital practices. It also introduces official
cybercrime reporting mechanisms.
